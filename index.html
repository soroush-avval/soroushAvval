Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Texas Tech University, researching UAV-enabled Mobile Edge Computing networks. My research interests lie at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and UAV Swarms. With a strong foundation in Electrical Engineering, I hold a Master's Degree in Electrical Engineering - Control from Sadjad University of Technology (2019). During my master's program, I focused on applied mathematics in Control Theory, designing control systems for Nonlinear and Complex systems. After graduating, I worked as an Artificial Intelligence Engineer, contributing to research published in prestigious journals and collaborating with the Part AI research center. I'm excited to continue exploring the possibilities of AI, ML, and UAVs in my Ph.D. research and beyond!
